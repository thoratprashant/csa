About Community Security App

All residential communities are born with the need to keep unwanted guests out. But how many gated communities actually keep track of who comes in and in trying to do so, how long does it takes your guest to come in?

CSA allows you to take control of your community making sure that each guest is actually invited and documented from even before arriving, making the entrance process much faster, organized and secure giving you many options and tools to keep your peace of mind at rest all the while knowing that your private information is kept private.

How It Works

1.Sign up and add community details

Register yourself on the website and use exciting features.

2.Upload residents and security guard info

Maintain the records of your community members and security guards in the same place.

3.Keep track of the visitor entry and exit logs

Give your residents the power to approve theirs guest and not rely on your guards guessing if they should or should not allow people in.

4.Broadcast messages to the community

Take control over your communications within your community targeting from one to all residents. Avoid having to search for personal information to contact a resident and do it thru a couple clicks of a button.
